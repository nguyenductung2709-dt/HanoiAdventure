Phase 1: Earn money
- Go west then south to go to Dong Da
- Enter Quoc Tu Giam
- Answer the questions
- You should have 100-20*4 = 20 staminas remaining

Phase 2: Enjoy cuisine
- Go north and enter Circle K at Ba Dinh to refill stamina.
- You should have 100 stamina
- Visit districts to enjoy their cuisines. Some districts don't have any cuisine, but you can buy drinks to refill you stamina there

Phase 3: Go home
- After enjoying all cuisines, go to Hoan Kiem and go home
